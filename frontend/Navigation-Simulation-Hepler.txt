Open the home page

## Home Page
- Search Stocks
- Read available stock ( For a Limit [10-15 No.s] )
- Can navigate to Stock creation , updation , deletion pages

## AddStock Page
- Can add stocks to inventory
- Can navigate to Home and  Stock updation , deletion pages

## UpdateStock Page
- Can update stock details in inventory
- Can navigate to Home and  Stock creation , deletion page

## DeleteStock Page
- Can delete stocks in inventory
- Can navigate to Home and  Stock creation , updation page
